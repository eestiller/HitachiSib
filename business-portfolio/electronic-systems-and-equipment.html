Electronic Systems and Equipment

Electronics have come to form the cornerstones of the manufacturing and healthcare fields, with Hitachi being a world leader in developing state-of-the-art electronic systems and equipment to meet numerous needs.

  • Medical Imaging Equipment

    Echelon Oval Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) Scanner

    Hitachi Medical’s Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) systems combine the company’s accumulated advanced MRI technology and know-how with cutting-edge technology development to deliver high-definition diagnostic images in an easy-to-operate package. Looking ahead, the company will continue to provide products that address customer needs to the medical frontline.

  • Test and Measurement Equipment

    Field Emission Scanning Electron Microscope (FE-SEM)

    Hitachi’s FE-SEMs have a broad range of high-resolution imaging applications, such as observing the structure of advanced nanotechnology material, and that of materials used in semiconductor devices, as well as other uses in pharmaceutical fields.

  • Semiconductor Manufacturing Systems

    QUIXACE Batch Thermal Process System for 300mm Wafers

    QUIXACE is Hitachi Kokusai’s latest platform for batch thermal processing of 300 mm wafers. It offers high throughput by utilizing advanced technologies in areas such as temperature control, wafer handling automation, reactor purging, and temperature ramping. We are proud to offer QUIXACE, the leading platform for today’s manufacturing environment which offers both flexibility and extendibility to meet tomorrow’s challenges.

Hitachi works on conserving the environment, by developing electric motors and inverters with low carbon dioxide emissions. Hitachi is also looking into DIG systems for smaller, more fuel-efficient engines.

Hitachi looks into your personal safety. In addition to control systems for brakes, suspension, and other sub-systems as well as car navigation systems, Hitachi also supplies high quality automotive parts such as carbon sliding materials and catalyst carriers to ensure automobiles operate at their best. To maintain the condition of automobiles, Hitachi provides support solutions such as testing equipment to determine if a bus or truck is at its peak performance.

Not just a manufacturer of automotive systems, Hitachi focuses on building a better relationship between vehicles and society, and intends to go further in bringing you the best for a comfortable and safer ride.
