SERVING THE WORLD WITH OUR SOCIAL INNOVATION BUSINESS

Social Infrastructure and Industrial System

Hitachi works towards sustainable development and enhanced comfort through various aspects of social infrastructure, such as the fields of manufacturing, water treatment and transportation.

  • Elevators and Escalators

    Elevators / Escalators / Moving sidewalks

    Hitachi offers high quality, technologically advanced and reliable elevators, escalators and other forms of vertical and horizontal transportation to Asia. We also retrofit such equipment to meet the needs of urban living.

  • Building Facilities Systems

    Industrial / Manufacturing / Commercial / Biomedical etc.

    Hitachi’s comprehensive range of products and services are available for various public, commercial and industrial application, such as air-conditioning, electrical, energy saving and solar systems.

  • Industrial Machinery

    Oil Flooded Screw Compressors (HISCREW Next Series

    Hitachi’s “NEXT” Series screw compressors are the latest advanced products for industrial use. Available features such as the inverter drive system helps contribute to power saving by varying power usage, depending on demand. Hitachi has both the Oil Free and Oil Flooded models to cater to various industries

  • Lightning Protection

    Dissipation Array System

    A revolutionary system that does not require an external power supply, Hitachi’s Dissipation Array System protects critical operations and facilities from damages and breakdowns caused by direct lightning strikes.

  • Railway Systems

    Monorail / Rolling Stock / Signaling etc.

    Based on the Alweg guideway straddle design, Hitachi’s monorail system is economical and safe, boasting a slim, versatile guideway that can be used in both hilly and developed urban areas. Sentosa Express is the first to use the small version of this system.

  • Water Plants

    Sea Water Reverse Osmosis (SWRO) Systems

    Hitachi Sea Water RO systems, available in various capacities, are designed to work efficiently and economically to produce high quality freshwater from seawater. This latest energy-saving technology ensures that high quality potable water can be produced economically from seawater.

  • Industrial Machinery

    Hitachi Ink Jet Printers

    Hitachi Ink Jet Printers are high quality, robust printers intended for industrial applications. The printer’s mode of operation is based on the Continuous Ink Jet (CIJ) principle. Our products are used for date coding, product marking and variable printing onto products used in everyday life.

Modern comforts and everyday conveniences are what make our lives easier. Hitachi serves people living in diverse urban environments through the building of strong public infrastructure that supports sustainable development and enhanced comfort.

Hitachi plays a multi-faceted role in social infrastructure, such as the areas of manufacturing, water treatment and transportation. One field that we are focusing on is water environmental solutions. For example, Hitachi has intelligent water systems that involve new water circulation systems that respond to water resource problems.

We also look into ultra high-speed, large capacity elevators for high-rise buildings, and efficient packaged air-conditioning systems to create comfortable environments for commercial and office use. Hitachi also takes an environmentally conscious approach to urban development by conceptualizing energy-efficient railway systems.

Hitachi also provides engineering solutions to protect critical facilities from the devastating effects of natural and accidental disasters which include lightning, earthquake and flooding. We have our eye on creating a better and brighter future in the long term. Hitachi is a social innovation business, and we will continue to push ourselves to deliver new ideas and improve the quality of our social infrastructure.
